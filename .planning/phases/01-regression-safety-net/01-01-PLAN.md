Required fixtures:

**numpy fixtures:**
- `sample_1d_float_array` — `np.array([1.0, 2.0, 3.0])`
- `sample_2d_float_array` — `np.random.rand(4, 3)` (shape (4,3))
- `sample_1d_int_array` — `np.array([1, 2, 3], dtype=int)`

**matplotlib fixtures** (all session-scoped to avoid repeated figure creation):
- `line2d_fixture` — creates fig/ax via `plt.subplots()`, calls `ax.plot([1,2,3],[4,5,6])`, yields `lines[0]` (a `Line2D`), closes figure in teardown
- `scatter2d_fixture` — `ax.scatter([1,2,3],[4,5,6])`, yields the `PathCollection`, closes figure
- `scatter3d_fixture` — `fig.add_subplot(111, projection='3d')`, calls `ax.scatter3D([1],[2],[3])`, yields the result, closes figure

**sklearn fixtures** (session-scoped):
- `fitted_kfold` — `KFold(n_splits=5)`
- `fitted_stratifiedkfold` — `StratifiedKFold(n_splits=5)`
- `fitted_shufflesplit` — `ShuffleSplit(n_splits=5, test_size=0.2)`
- `fitted_svc` — `SVC()` fitted on `[[0,0],[1,1]], [0,1]`
- `fitted_gridsearchcv` — `GridSearchCV(SVC(), {'C': [1, 10]})` fitted on `[[0,0],[1,1],[2,2],[3,3]], [0,1,0,1]`
- `fitted_randomforestclassifier` — `RandomForestClassifier(n_estimators=10, random_state=42)` fitted on `[[0,0],[1,1],[2,2],[3,3]], [0,1,0,1]`
- `fitted_decisiontreeclassifier` — `DecisionTreeClassifier()` fitted on `[[0,0],[1,1],[2,2],[3,3]], [0,1,0,1]`
- `fitted_logisticregression` — `LogisticRegression()` fitted on `[[0,0],[1,1],[2,2],[3,3]], [0,1,0,1]`

All sklearn imports must be inside the fixture functions or after a top-level `try/except ImportError` guard so that the file does not crash if sklearn is somehow absent (it should be present after Task 1).
